Autumn Gardening Tips!

This is the best season to start pruning your shrubs and berry bushes just before winter. It is crucial to do this to improve the lateral growth of bushes, ensures good airflow, and reduces disease risk. This also avoids the nesting seasons for birds.

Make sure your compost bin is full for next season! It will likely be at the brim if you had a busy season with lots of crops grown and ready to add to the heap! Make sure that there is a combination of green (green leaves and grass) and brown (dry leaves, shredded cardboard) compostable waste.
